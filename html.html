How It's Run!

Step 1

First, you tell us all about your program and your kids. We talk about what game you or your program want and pricing.

Step 2

Next, our staff will come to your facility to learn your space and layout. Next we come out to take measurements for game set up. This will help us to create the best game for you.

Step 3

Then on your scheduled event date, we come out and set up everything! We will bring all of the equipment needed. Our staff will give a brief demonstration of the ACTIONS necessary for the game then we have an ADVENTURE!

Step 4

Last, we clean, pack up and leave!

Our Mission

“Action and Adventure was ultimately created for kids to be a certain character, and play in a controlled atmosphere. It’s no different from kids on a playground pretending to be there favorite Super Hero.... Or Villan!!”
